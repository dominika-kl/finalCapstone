Across the Ocean

21 states, 9000 km, almost 3 weeks - what a road trip! Milo was very brave and excited, sniffing around and meeting new friends all over the US. The weather was hot, many beaches were not pet friendly due to wildlife, but we had lots of fun! We started our trip in Montreal, headed to Maine, Massachusetts and then took a short break in New York (not the best place for dogs - too busy and loud). Boston was much better! We also had a chance to stop in Salem, widely noted for the infamous Salem witch trials. Next step was Philadelphia, where Milo did a quick run up the Rocky Balboa steps and had a Philly Cheesesteak sandwich. In Washington Milo took a selfie in front of the Capitol, next we enjoyed the views of Shenandoah National Park and then we took a couple of nice walks Charleston we took. Virginia, North Carolina, South Carolina and Georgia and finally Florida! Kennedy Space Center in Cape Canaveral offers an amazing kennel for your dog (free of charge!), when you visit the site. Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Key West,xCape Coral, Tampa, Pensacola..These are just some of the places we've been to. In Florida, you will find the most amazing white sandy beaches, even special beaches for doggies! We had a chacne to meet a very polite dolphin when we plunged into the bay at one of those! Everglade National Park is a must see, watch out on gators though! Especially when walking your dog near water. Luisiana with New Orleans and Mississippi where quite an adventure. A bit too hot for Milo! He needed to cool down in the shade quite often and he drunk plenty of water during our walks.
